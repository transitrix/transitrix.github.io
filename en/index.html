Autonomous organisations

Transitrix is not a company — it is a private initiative where I study how organisations can behave more autonomously: sensing their environment, adapting structure and behaviour, and remaining coherent without constant central steering.

What this is

The work lives in three connected projects — each probes a different layer, from executable enterprise models and multi-agent dynamics to governance systems that let an organisation reshuffle itself when conditions change.

Projects

Transitrix DSM

A system for organisational management that helps a body dynamically adapt to its external context and internal state — coupling sensing, decisions, and structural change rather than freezing design in static charts.

Swarm-platform

A space to explore multi-agent systems: model interactions, emergence, and coordination patterns so intuition about swarms can be grounded in repeatable experiments.

LiteEA

A lightweight EA methodology for shaping and steering an enterprise model through Architecture as Code — keeping architecture legible, versioned, and close to implementation reality.
